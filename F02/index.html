SAM INFOGRAPHICS

We are a dedicated team of researchers working tirelessly to expose the critical issue of cyberbullying among students. Through comprehensive surveys conducted within the PUPSTC community, we aimed to gather insightful perspectives on how social media platforms impact their experiences with cyberbullying and hate speech.

Our infographic, titled 'BLOCKED KA SA'KIN, SIS!: PUPSTC Students' Take on Cyberbullying and Hate Speech Challenges in Social Media Platforms,' presents the findings that shed light on the alarming effects of online harassment. We delve into the various dimensions of this pressing issue, including the prevalence of cyberbullying, the psychological implications it fosters, and the collective stance of students on fostering a healthier online environment.

Join us in raising awareness and championing proactive solutions to combat hate speech and cyberbullying in our digital community. Together, we can create a safer and more supportive online space for everyone.
